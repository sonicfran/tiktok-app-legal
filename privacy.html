Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Travel Video Bot with TikTok, we access your basic profile information (user ID) and permission to publish videos on your behalf. We do not collect or store any additional personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok authorization tokens are stored locally on your device and used solely to publish travel guide videos to your TikTok account.

3. Data Storage

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on your machine. No data is transmitted to third-party servers other than TikTok's API for video publishing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can revoke access at any time by removing the application from your TikTok account settings. Local token files can be deleted from your device.

6. TikTok API

This application complies with TikTok's Developer Terms and Privacy Policy.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For questions, open an issue at github.com/SonicWZRD/tiktok-app-legal.
